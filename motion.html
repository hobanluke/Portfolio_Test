Information
Luke attended New Zealand’s Massey University’s College of Creative Arts, graduating in 2017 with Honours. Here he made his mark in the design industry, receiving multiple DINZ Best Awards and featured in It’s Nice That’s yearly graduates. Outside of university he worked on a range of freelance projects, with clients including Weta Workshop and the Ministry of Primary Industries.

Luke made the move to Sydney in 2018 to join MAUD, where he worked on a variety of projects and rebrands within the cultural and corporate sector, specialising in translating visual identities into motion and digital experiences. Notable projects include, Sydney Dance Company, Google Rare, Kaldor Public Art Project and Australia House.

In 2021, Luke joined the New Zealand based studio, 1/1. Working with many international clients in the fashion and beauty industries, he played a pivotal role in leading the studio’s digital and motion projects. During this time, he formalised a process of delivering clear design systems and specifications to developers and clients. Notable projects include, Yu Mei, REOME, Hyeja [惠慈], Flowers, Danny Kaplan Studio and BAINA.

If you would like more information or to discuss commissions, collaborations, or other opportunities, please don't hesitate to get in touch via email.
